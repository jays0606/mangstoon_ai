## Problem Statements (Pick One)

### 1. Gemini in Entertainment
Leverage Google's AI products for breakthrough experiences in music, TV, film, and gaming. Korea/Seoul is a global entertainment powerhouse.

### 2. Gemini in Hard Tech
Build with Gemini to accelerate hardware, biotech, data centers, energy, and aerospace.

### 3. Gemini for Good
Use Google's AI suite for better social, economic, or cultural outcomes.

## Rules

- **Team Size:** Up to 4 members
- **New Work Only** — no presenting existing projects as your own (immediate DQ)
- **Demo must clearly highlight** features/code built during the hackathon (immediate DQ otherwise)
- May use closed-source models/APIs (including Google's) if accessible to all hackers
- Can build on top of existing open source projects

### Banned Projects (DQ)

- AI Mental Health Advisor
- Basic RAG Applications
- Streamlit Applications
- Image Analyzers
- "AI for Education" Chatbot
- AI Job Application Screener
- AI Nutrition Coach
- Personality Analyzers
- Any project giving medical advice

## Judging Criteria

| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| **Demo** — Does it work? How well implemented? | 50% |
| **Impact** — Long-term potential, usefulness, fit to problem statement | 25% |
| **Creativity** — Innovative concept, unique demo | 15% |
| **Pitch** — How effectively presented | 10% |

### Judging Process
- Round 1: ~3 min pitch + 1-2 min Q&A per group
- Round 2: Top 6 teams demo on stage, ~3 min pitch + 2-3 min Q&A
